Master basic skills

"Being a newcomer to Party animals, knowing more basic information about the operation helps to win."

Tip 1:In a fight with an opponent, the environment can be used to defeat the opponent or briefly stun the opponent. In "Ichiban", you can lift your opponent up and throw him into a green poisonous gas.

Tip 2:Beware of hidden traps in different maps. For example, "Winter Is Coming" will randomly refresh the bomb, if you do not escape in time, the bomb will take you away from the campfire, you will lose the ability to keep out of the cold.

Tip 3:Use weapons scattered throughout the arena, you can use them to attack your opponents, but use them properly or you will hurt yourself.

Tip 4:Don't forget that teamwork is the most important part of this game, and teammates can help you out at key moments.
