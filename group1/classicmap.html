Unlock the carnival world of
Party animals

Party Animals have a total of 20 maps, divided into three different modes-"Last stand" "Team score" "Arcade".

Each map is constantly shuffled at the end of each round, allowing players to experience different maps as they play.Players can play random maps by matching random players or with friends (up to 3 people), or they can play maps and rules of their choice with friends (up to 7 people) in custom matches.

Last stand

  • Ichiban: In an ancient Japanese ruins, poisonous green gas gradually covers the map as the battle progresses.

  • Typhoon: In crazy typhoons, watch out for sinking submarines.

  • Winter Is Coming: The campfires are going out one by one. Don't leave the campfire.

  • Broken Arrow: An aircraft is gradually freezing, avoid staying within the ice range.

  • Blackhole Lab: When the black hole opens, it is necessary to grasp the chain or object in the map to avoid being swept away.

  • Wind Tunnel: Before the attack of the wind, smoothly find the lifting door to avoid the wind.

  • Beat-Up Bridge: To be a survivor of a broken bridge.

  • Gator Valley:The bridge will experience huge waves and be the last survivor.

  • Ice Breaker:To be the last survivor of a broken iceberg.

Team score

  • Safely Afloat: Get a floating safe to win.

  • Fluffy Redemption: Complete the assigned task on parallel trains
