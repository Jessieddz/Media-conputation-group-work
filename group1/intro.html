Unlock the carnival world of Party animals

Party Animals is a multi-player fighting game developed by Chinese studio Recreate Games.

In it, you can be the cutest animal you can imagine, but don't be fooled by their charming appearance, because they are full of fighting power. Party animals offers different game modes and lots of crazy maps for you to use.

Designed to offer players more fun than competition, the game features fights between cute stuffed animals that are especially good for engaging with friends.
